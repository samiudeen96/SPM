Product Features

Cattleview is a brand-new product geared toward cow farmers that provides real-time data on animal count, movement/behavior, and health metrics such as temperature. It can help spot preventable health issues in cattle with early detection and saves a significant amount of time tracking inventory - Product specs claim to reduce hours spent on animal counting by 95%.

Cattleview is unable to fly in extreme weather conditions.

Cattleview's main competitors are similar drones from JBS that cost less ($1,000/month) but lacks some of the features like health metrics. Your job is to create a unique, creative value proposition by first identifying value drivers then calculating the value of the most important value drivers, and then communicating your value proposition to your prospective client, Supreme Pastures.

Customer Facts
  • Cattle count peformed 2x daily, which requires 2 hours of labor total. Cattle counter is paid $20/hour. This is done 365 days a year
  • They have a monthly deadline to turn in cattle counts, or else they are fined $1,000. Late counts happen on average, 3 months each year
  • About half the days, there isn't work to do for everyone, so there are about 20 hours of unaccounted work on those days
  • Daily, about 20% of pens don't need food replenishment, but it's different every time. It takes about 2 hours each day to do this job
  • There are about 30 extreme weather events yearly when the drone wouldn't be able to fly. They may have to pay someone overtime (4x) to come in those days & count
  • Having real time data on cow health could help client catch disease early, reducing loss by .01%
